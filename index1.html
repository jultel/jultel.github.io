About me

Hi! I'm Yuliia Telishchuk, landcape designer and I'm a student of NLTU. In addition, my interest in photography began a few years ago when I got my first camera. Why do I enjoy taking pictures? Because when you look at the light through the camera lens, you notice moments that you would otherwise payed your attention to in a hurry, as we always do. The picture allows you to stop and see the beauty of this world, nature, man, life. So why not capture this beautiful moment in the photo? I love to show people through their photos their sincerity, beauty, emotions, their uniqueness and, perhaps, even what they have not noticed in themselves. I'm an amateur-level photograhper. I never expect praise at my own expense, I expect criticism and I perceive it. In the future, I want to devote a lot of time to this fascinating and interesting art.
